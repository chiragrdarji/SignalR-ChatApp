TRA will provide access to TRA’s proprietary software IndustrySafe, including a browser interface and data encryption, transmission, access and storage. IndustrySafe is safety management software designed to manage safety activity, including the modules listed below:

Home. With the Home Module, users can schedule and view all of their related safety activities, including tasks, events, inspections, corrective actions and training. The Home Module serves as a compliance calendar alerting users to upcoming and overdue safety items.

Corrective Actions.The Corrective Actions Module enables organizations to generate plans for incidents, hazards, safety inspections, security activities and observations. Tracking the assignment and status of problem reports enables timely completion of corrective action plans and improved safety. Automatic e-mail alerts notify key responsible parties of open, overdue and closed corrective actions.

Hazards. The Hazards Module enables organizations to identify, analyze and remediate hazardous conditions. Risk assessments based on probability and severity can be conducted for each hazard identified within the organization. Automatic e-mail alerts notify users of hazards.
